GetMyLocations — Real-time Location Finder

Find My Location — IP & GPS Coordinates Finder

GetMyLocations is a free, privacy-first online tool that detects your current location and displays your exact latitude, longitude, city, and country on an interactive map. Use it as a fast latitude and longitude finder, IP location tracker, or live GPS coordinates lookup — all in one place, 100% in your browser.

What you can do with GetMyLocations

  • Find my location instantly using IP, Wi-Fi, and GPS signals
  • View precise latitude and longitude in WGS-84 decimal degrees
  • Reverse-geocode any coordinates into city, region, and country
  • Enter coordinates manually to fly the map to any place on Earth
  • Copy GPS coordinates to clipboard with one click
  • Track my IP location and GPS in real time as you move

How to find your current location

  1. Open the GetMyLocations location finder.
  2. Approve the one-time browser geolocation permission prompt.
  3. Your latitude, longitude, accuracy, city, and country appear instantly on the dashboard and interactive map.

Frequently asked questions

How do I find my current location online?

Open GetMyLocations, allow the browser's location permission, and your latitude, longitude, city, and country appear instantly on an interactive map.

What is my IP location?

IP location is an approximate position derived from your network address. GetMyLocations combines IP, Wi-Fi, and GPS signals for the most accurate result.

How do I find latitude and longitude?

GetMyLocations displays live GPS coordinates in WGS-84 decimal degrees. You can also enter coordinates manually to fly the map to any location.

Is GetMyLocations free and private?

Yes. GetMyLocations is 100% free, requires no signup, and runs fully in your browser — your coordinates are never stored or shared.
