Free Tool · United States Map

Interactive map of the United States

Search any US city or address, click anywhere on the country to drop a pin and read the GPS coordinates, or use your phone’s location to centre the map on where you are. Free, no signup, no API key.

What this map does

The map opens centred on the continental United States at a zoom level where the outline of all 48 contiguous states is visible at once. Zoom in for state, county, and city detail. The basemap is OpenStreetMap rendered through CARTO’s Voyager style, so place names, highways, and city outlines all show clearly without the visual noise of a full satellite view. For satellite imagery of the same area, switch to the Satellite tool.

Three things you can do here

  • Find any US location. Type a city, ZIP code, or full street address and the map jumps to it.
  • Read coordinates anywhere. Click on the map to drop a pin. The exact latitude and longitude appear below the map.
  • Centre on your current location. Tap the “Use my location” button and the map jumps to your GPS position, if you allow the browser’s location prompt.
